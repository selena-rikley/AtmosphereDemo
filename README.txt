Demo
This application is a demo of how simple things about the environment can influence a player's experience of a game. 


Controls:

Toggle Skybox : LEFT SHIFT + (1 or 2 or 3 or 4 or 5)
Togle Lgihts : LEFT ALT + (1 or 2 or 3 or 4 or 5)

Toggle Background Music : LEFT ALT + (F1 or F2 or F3 or F4 or F5)
Toggle Atmospheric/Ambient Sounds : LEFT SHIFT + (F1 or F2 or F3 or F4 or F5)
Play Various Alert Sounds :  LEFT CTRL + (F1 or F2 or F3 or F4 or F5)

Move Around : WASD
Look Around : Mouse
